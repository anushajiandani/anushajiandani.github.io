About

Anusha Jiandani is a freelance Visual Artist who was born in the Caribbean island of St.Vincent & the Grenadines. While being able to experience a variety of cultural backgrounds; from island life to 3 years residing in South India for boarding school, she developed a growing love for drawing and photography. She specializes in illustration, design and photography. Anusha is currently residing in Miami, Florida where she is pursuing her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Photography. Her future goals consist of being able to cultivate and grow her brand and keep creating as a freelance visual artist.
